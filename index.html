⚡ Real-World: Power Surges

In electrical engineering, limits are used to analyze how voltage behaves as time approaches the moment a switch is turned on. As time gets closer to the switching point, the voltage approaches different values from before and after the connection. By evaluating these limits, engineers can understand and predict sudden changes in voltage levels, even when the exact value at that instant is not defined.
